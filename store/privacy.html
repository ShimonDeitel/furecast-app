Privacy Policy

Furecast — Pet Cost Predictor · Last updated July 2026

Furecast is built to keep as little as possible off your device. This page describes, honestly and specifically, what happens to your data.

What stays on your device

Every pet profile, breed prediction, and expense you log is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Furecast has no user account, no server database, and no sign-in. If you delete the app, or use the in-app "Erase All Data" option, that data is gone — Furecast has no copy anywhere else.

What is sent over the network

Furecast makes network requests in exactly two situations:

  • AI surprise-risk coaching (Pro feature): when you tap "Get AI Insights," Furecast sends your pet's species, selected breed, approximate age, and a summary of your logged expense categories (for example, "Food: $120, Grooming: $60") to a Cloudflare Workers AI service that generates the coaching reply. This request does not include your name, device identifier, location, or any other personal identifier — only the pet and expense details described above.
  • App Store purchases: subscribing to Furecast Pro is handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit and App Store systems, under Apple's own privacy policy. Furecast never sees your payment details.

How the AI request is handled

The AI coaching request is processed by a stateless Cloudflare Worker that generates a reply and does not retain, log to a database, or link the request to you or your device in any way. There is no user account on the other end and no way to look up past requests. Abuse is limited by a shared, anonymous rate limit rather than by identifying who is asking.

What Furecast does not do

  • No advertising, no ad SDKs, no third-party analytics or tracking.
  • No selling or sharing of your data with anyone.
  • No location access, no contacts access, no photo library access.
  • No account creation, sign-in, or password of any kind.

Your control

You can erase every pet, prediction, and logged expense at any time from Settings → Erase All Data. Because nothing is stored server-side, this is a complete, immediate, and irreversible deletion.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same address with a new "last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to the developer via the App Store support contact listed on Furecast's App Store page.
